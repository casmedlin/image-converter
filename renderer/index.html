Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 23, 2026


Image Converter ("the App") is a desktop application that processes images entirely on your device. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.


1. No Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, usage statistics, or analytics. All image processing occurs locally on your device. No images, metadata, or derived files are ever sent to any external server.


2. Local Processing Only

All image conversion, resizing, and format transformation is performed using local system tools (macOS sips/ImageIO or Electron/Node.js libraries). The App has no network access and no cloud components.


3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. It operates fully offline and does not require an internet connection.


4. Data Storage

Temporary files created during processing are stored in your system's temporary directory and are deleted immediately after processing completes. Output files are saved only to the location you explicitly choose.


5. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the effective date at the top will be revised. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of any changes.
